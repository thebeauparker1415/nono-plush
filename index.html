How Nono Came to Be

Nono is the creation of a woman named Celia Thorne. Stitched with love and care, and some wonderous magic, Nono was born from a simple idea: to create a plush companion that would bring joy and comfort to those who needed it most. Celia has made many other plush toys, but none quite like Nono.
